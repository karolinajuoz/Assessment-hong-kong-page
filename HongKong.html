HongKong, China- Pearl of the Orient

About HongKong:

HongKong is best known for its vibrant nightlife and rich culture. It has the highest number of skyscrapers in the world and is a "Global Business Hub" (3rd ranked global finance centre). It is a fusion between traditional history and modernity- the tightly packed city has it all.

My experience:

I spent two unforgettable weeks in Hong Kong during Chinese New Year in 2021, and it was an experience of a lifetime. The city was alive with vibrant festivities, colorful parades, and spectacular fireworks. I immersed myself in the local culture, joining the traditional lion dances and savoring festive delicacies like dim sum and mooncakes. Hong Kong during Chinese New Year is a celebration of life and culture that everyone should experience at least once.
